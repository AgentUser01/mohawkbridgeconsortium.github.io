Mohawk Bridge Consortium's Expertise

Deck Replacement

From 2008 to 2016, MBC replaced the entire deck of the federal portion of the Honoré Mercier Bridge. As the design-build contractor, MBC completed Canada’s largest bridge restoration in history. Since 2016, MBC has continually worked on the Mercier Bridge in other aspects of rehabilitation.

Sandblast & Paint

Mohawk Bridge Consortium specializes in the essential services of sandblasting and painting bridge structures. Our expertise encompasses all phases of bridge restoration—from meticulous surface preparation through to final, protective paint application. The Mohawk Bridge Consortium collaborates with clients to deliver durable, industry-standard, paint systems, ensuring each job not only meets aesthetic standards but also withstands the rigours of its environment, thus maintaining safety and structural integrity. MBC is the second largest sandblast and paint company in Canada.

Steelworks

We specialize in the installation of bridge steel components. In the role of design-build or conventional contractor, we oversee all aspects of steel installation from working with industry-leading fabricators to the actual installation of all components big or small. MBC's History

A proud history of success

Mohawk Bridge Consortium (MBC) traces its roots back to the historic Kahnawake Territory Land Claim Settlement of 1717, which granted the Mohawk people exclusive rights to maintain and operate bridges spanning the St. Lawrence River.

Guided by the core Mohawk principles of respect, responsibility, and perseverance, MBC takes immense pride in delivering projects of the highest caliber while fostering strong relationships with clients, partners, and the local community. Through its unwavering commitment to quality and its deep-rooted connection to the land, MBC continues to honor the legacy entrusted to the Mohawk people generations ago.
